Mojo Pork

Description

Unlike revenge, Jambalaya is a dish best served hot. But, like revenge, jambalaya is best when you use anything and everything at your disposal. So there really is no set rule as far as ingredients except whatever "gets the job done" also like revenge...

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Chicken
  • Shrimp
  • Chicken stock
  • Onion
  • Bacon
  • Celery
  • Bell pepper
  • Minced garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Brown rice

Steps

  1. Brown Bacon
  2. Add onion to caramalize
  3. Add Chicken
  4. Add Bell peppers and celery
  5. Add tomatoes and chicken stock
  6. Add garlic
